Profile

Twenty years at the sharp end.

I spent the hardest, most rewarding years of my career in 24/7 L3 support — the deepest point in the chain, where the billing, rating, and charging platforms either stay up or the business stops.

Over 20+ years I’ve moved from writing the middleware fixes myself to leading the teams that prevent the incident in the first place — sustaining 99.9%+ availability across mission-critical telecom and retail platforms, and cutting major outages by 82% year over year.

That combination is the rare part: genuine hands-on depth in enterprise application support, plus the people leadership, ITIL governance, and Agile delivery to turn reactive firefighting into engineered reliability.

Today I’m focused on modernizing enterprise support with AI — from predictive incident analysis to AI-built tooling — and looking for a senior management role anchored back in the L3 world I do best.
